About Our App

Kinu Seller is a video-first e-commerce marketplace in Nepal designed specifically for sellers. With Kinu Seller, anyone can effortlessly showcase and sell a wide range of products through engaging short videos. By leveraging the power of videos, Kinu Seller aims to make the selling experience enjoyable and convenient, enabling seamless product discovery for potential customers.
